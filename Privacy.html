Privacy Policy for Criminal Keyword Spotting App (KWS)

This Privacy Policy outlines how information is collected, used, and disclosed by the Criminal Keyword Spotting application ("KWS").

By using the KWS, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use:

  • Keyword Recognition: KWS recognizes keywords associated with criminal activities from user interactions within the application.
  • Timestamp and Date: KWS captures the timestamp and date when keywords linked to criminal activities are identified for the purpose of logging and analysis.

Data Security:

  • Encryption: All data transmission within KWS is encrypted to ensure the security of information.
  • Secure Storage: User data, including timestamps and detected keywords, is stored securely on our servers with appropriate safeguards against unauthorized access.

Information Sharing:

  • Education Purposes: Detected keywords and associated metadata may be used for educational purposes, such as research or analysis, to better understand trends in criminal activities. The KWS app is purely for educational purposes.
  • Third Parties: We do not share user data with third parties except as required by law.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes through KWS or other appropriate channels.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@Ckws.com.
